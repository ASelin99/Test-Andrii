The press center is a place for holding press conferences, briefings, Internet conferences, round tables, hotlines, video bridges. The press center hall is equipped with modern multimedia equipment - TV splitters, plasma panels, web cameras, mSicrophones for speakers and journalists, inputs for connecting digital voice recorders to the conference system, telephone communication, Wi-Fi network. The hall of the press center has 52 seats for journalists. There is a possibility of Internet broadcasting of press center events online.
