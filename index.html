Kharkiv

10 interesting facts about Kharkiv:

1. Kharkov - the largest city on 50 parallels

Kharkov is not considered the largest city in Ukraine and neighboring countries, but if you draw an invisible line of 50 parallel, it is really the largest. In honor of this, in October 2010 in the garden of Shevchenko was installed a kind of monument, reflecting the intersection of the city with this parallel. In the center of the sign is a bronze circle with a diameter of 2 meters with a map and plotted distances from Kharkov to various cities of the world, and the latitude is denoted by a broken line of 20 copper plates.

2. The first "skyscraper" in the USSR

Gosprom - is the first skyscraper (multi-storey reinforced concrete building) in the USSR, built in 1928. There are still used and working elevators from those times. Erected unusually «tall» for those times building in record time - only 2.5 years. The height of the Gosprom is 63 m, and together with the TV tower installed in 1955 - 108 m.

3. Barabashov market - the largest market in Europe

Kharkiv market in the area of the metro academician Barabashov is considered the largest market in Eastern Europe today and ranks 14th in the ranking of the world’s largest markets. Its area is 75 hectares.

4. The largest area of Ukraine

Freedom Square in Kharkov is one of the largest in all of Europe, because it occupies a whole 12 hectares. The layout is amazing scale, and it is here that the largest concerts and events are held.

5. The oldest Zoo in Ukraine

Moreover, the Kharkiv Zoo, founded in 1895, is one of the oldest zoos in Europe. Over the years, it has been rebuilt, expanded and rebuilt several times after the war. In 1980, the zoo was granted the status of a protected area.

6. The biggest free concert in Ukraine

In 2008 in Kharkiv on Freedom Square gathered 350 thousand people for a concert of the legendary group Queen. Rock musicians did not receive a penny for performing, as the concert was free, but it entered the list of the most crowded concerts in Ukraine.

7. The first children’s railway in Kharkiv

Between the cinema «Park» and the Forest Park in 1940 the first children’s railway was built, which was named «Small Yuzhny». This is a unique narrow-gauge road on which children work as guides, drivers and passengers - also children. Young workers undergo special training in the building at «Small South».

8. Saltov Housing Complex

Saltov residential complex Kharkov - the largest «sleeping area» in Ukraine, the population of which is more than 400 thousand people.

9. Kharkiv Disneyland

The Central City Park in recent years has become almost the main attraction of the city. After reconstruction, it probably has no competitors in Ukraine and is not inferior to the best world analogues. This is the most modern and most beautiful recreation and entertainment park in the country.

10. Annunciation Cathedral

The Blagoveshchensk Cathedral in Kharkov is the largest cathedral in eastern Europe. The first thing you will notice is his luxury: the five domes of the temple, built in the neo-Byzantine style in 1901, are simply striking! The attention is also paid to the unsurpassed arch at the entrance to the building, four majestic columns and rich stucco decoration.
